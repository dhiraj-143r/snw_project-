Care that's always there, for you and your family

Maven is the leading digital health platform for women's and family health, providing expert care across every life stage.

Learn our story

Whether you're planning or raising a family, we're here for you

Maven was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to transform healthcare for women and families worldwide. We recognized a critical gap in the healthcare system – a lack of specialized, accessible, and comprehensive care for women's health needs.

What started as a platform connecting women with fertility experts has grown into the world's largest digital health platform for women's and family health, serving millions of members across 175+ countries.

Maven is for everyone – regardless of your family structure, background, or healthcare journey. We're committed to providing inclusive care that meets you where you are.

Maven members experience life changing moments

Since our founding, Maven has helped millions of people navigate their healthcare journeys with confidence and support. Our impact extends beyond individual health outcomes to create systemic change in how women's and family healthcare is delivered.

Through our platform, we've reduced maternity complications by 25%, increased fertility success rates by 30%, and helped members save an average of $2,500 per year on healthcare costs.
